Green Fruit Salad

Salad for accompanys of other dish, how mealts and fishs

Ingredients

  • ½ chopped onion
  • ½ cup chopped green bell pepper
  • 2(10 ounce)packages mixed salad greens
  • 4 thinly sliced chicken deli meat, chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • ¼ teaspoon onion powder
  • 3 dashes garlic powder
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 2 pinched salt
  • 3 tablespoon balsamic vinaigrette salad dressing

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Microwave or saute onion and bell pepper until soft; set aside to cool.

  2. Step 2

    3. In a large salad bowl, combine the onion, pepper, salad greens, deli meat and tomato. Sprinkle with the onion powder, garlic powder, black pepper and salt. Toss to mix.

  3. Step 3

    4. Pour on enough salad dressing or vinegar to coat, toss again and serve.
